Peach Lemonade


Peach lemonade, a peach drink from the south, makes an easy fix for a hot day.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup white sugar;
  • 7 cups water, divided;
  • 1 ¼ cups freshly squeezed lemon juice;
  • 2 fresh peaches, peeled and pitted;
  • a blender;
  • sliced peaches for garnish (optional);
  • ice as needed.

Directions

  1. Place sugar in a saucepan. Pour 1 cup water. Turn heat to medium and stir continuously until sugar granules are dissolved. Remove from heat and let cool.
  2. Pour lemon juice into a pitcher. Pour in remaining 6 cups water.

  4. Adjust to taste by adding more water or sugar. Add garnish such as a slice of peach, ice, and serve.
